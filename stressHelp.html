Dealing with Stress:

Hello highschoolers! Highschool can be very scary and stressful, but here at GuideMe we prioritze stress free environments and express validation towards academic help. We want to show fellow high schoolors that high school doesnt have to be stressful but fun filled and energy based!!

Here are some amazing resources and tips/tricks to help highschoolors with stress!

  1. Sleep Well
  2. Exercise
  3. Talk it out
  4. Set aside time for fun
  5. Get outside

These are only a few of the many ways teenagers and people of all ages can practice healthy stress management techniques. It offers a safe and enjoyable way to relieve yourself, while relieving the things that cause so much harm in the lives of the youth.

Strategies to control stressful situations:

  1. Tackling the Problem:
      2. Identifying and addressing the problem
      Brainstorming ways to work around it
  2. Taking Care of your Health:
      3. Exercising regularly
      Taking time to relax
      Eating well
      Getting lots of sleep
  3. Dealing with Emotions:
  4. Take time off of work and school
  5. Release your emotions to someone trusted
      6. Contribute to a greater cause
  6. Contribute to your community
